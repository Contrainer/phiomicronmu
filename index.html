PHI OMICRON MU

Welcome to the official website of Phi Omicron Mu!

ΦΟΜ is a co-ed fraternity on the campus of American University in Washington, DC. We do not charge dues. A minimum GPA of 2.5 each semester is required for continuous membership.

Our philanthropic efforts are to fund education in swimming and Model UN for underprivileged children in the DC area.

We are committed to diversity, equity, and inclusion.

For any questions or concerns please contact Fred Gordon at fg3756a@american.edu.
