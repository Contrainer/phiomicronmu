PHI OMICRON MU

Welcome to the official website of Phi Omicron Mu!

ΦΟΜ is a fraternity on the campus of American University in Washington, DC. We do not charge dues. A minimum GPA of 2.5 each semester is required for continuous membership.

Our philanthropic efforts are to fund swim school for underprivileged children in the DC area.
